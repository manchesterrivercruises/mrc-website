There is nowhere spookier — or more fun — to spend Halloween than out on the dark water. The Halloween Boat Party turns the Manchester Ship Canal into a floating fright night, with a DJ, a licensed bar and a boatful of ghouls, witches and questionable costumes ready to dance until the witching hour.

As the boat slips out from Salford Quays into the gloom, the DJ conjures up the party anthems and the night takes hold. Fog on the water, the city lights glinting eerily off the canal, a dancefloor full of vampires and zombies throwing shapes — it is a brilliantly atmospheric setting for a big night out, and the theming makes it feel like a proper occasion. Costumes are optional, but frankly you'll wish you'd made the effort when you see everyone else's.

With a fully licensed bar keeping the potions flowing and Manchester's waterfront looming spookily past the windows, the mood is equal parts party and playful horror. It is a fantastic night for a birthday that happens to fall near the 31st, a group of friends who love an excuse to dress up, or anyone who takes their Halloween seriously.

Come as your scariest, silliest or most spectacular self — there may even be a prize for the best costume — and dance the spooky season away on the water. Just keep an eye on the shadows...

Our Halloween party sells out fast every year, so round up your coven and book ahead. We'll see you after dark.
