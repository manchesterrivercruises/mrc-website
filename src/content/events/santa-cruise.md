Climb aboard for the most magical trip of the festive season. Our Cruise with Father Christmas takes families along the Manchester Ship Canal in a warm, twinkling boat, where little ones can meet the big man himself and feel the full sparkle of Christmas on the water.

From the moment you step aboard at Salford Quays, the festive spirit takes over. The cabin is snug and decorated for the season, seasonal music fills the air, and there is a real sense of anticipation as the boat sets off. The highlight, of course, is a visit from Father Christmas, who meets every child aboard and hands out a special gift — a moment of pure wonder that parents treasure as much as the children do.

It is a gentle, easy outing pitched perfectly for young families: the right length for little ones, no long queues, and plenty of magic packed into every minute. Refreshments are available onboard, and the ever-changing waterfront makes a lovely backdrop as you cruise. For children with additional needs, we also run a relaxed, sensory-friendly SEN Santa cruise.

Our Santa cruises are one of the most popular things we do all year, and they sell out quickly — so book early to avoid disappointment.

Want to know more before you book? Read our full [Santa Cruise guide](/santa-cruise-manchester) for everything the festive season has to offer on the water.
