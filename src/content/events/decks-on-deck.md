Who says the best parties happen after dark? Decks on Deck flips the script with a daytime DJ session on the Manchester Ship Canal — sunshine, house music and open-air dancing with the whole city as your backdrop.

This is a party with real festival energy. As the boat cruises out from Salford Quays, the DJ eases in with warm, rolling house before building through the afternoon into full-on floor-filler territory. With the deck open to the sky and the water sparkling around you, there is a freedom to a daytime party you just don't get indoors — sunglasses on, drink in hand, moving to the beat as Manchester's waterfront drifts by.

With a fully licensed bar onboard and that unbeatable open-air setting, the vibe is pure, easy joy. It is a brilliant choice for a birthday, a summer catch-up or a group who want their party with a side of sunshine and skyline. Come for the music, stay for the feeling of dancing on the water in broad daylight.

There is something special about a daytime rave on a boat — it feels like a proper occasion and a mini-holiday all at once. And because it wraps up while the day is still young, there is plenty of night left for wherever the group heads next.

Decks on Deck is one of our most popular daytime events and tickets sell fast, so grab your crew and book ahead. Sun's out, decks out — we'll see you on the water.
