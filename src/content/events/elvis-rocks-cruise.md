Ladies and gentlemen — the King has left the building, and he's boarded a boat on the Manchester Ship Canal. Elvis Rocks is a night of pure rock 'n' roll swagger, where the collars are turned up, the lip is curled, and nobody stays in their seat for long.

This is Elvis at full throttle. From the first snarling guitar riff, our tribute takes you straight back to the golden age of rock 'n' roll — the leg-shaking, the jailhouse jive, the ballads that still give you goosebumps. "Hound Dog", "Blue Suede Shoes", "Suspicious Minds", "Can't Help Falling in Love" — the hits keep coming as the boat cruises out into the night.

There's a raw energy to an Elvis night that's impossible to resist. Before long you'll be up on your feet, jiving with the group and singing along to every word, while the fully licensed bar keeps the rock 'n' roll spirit flowing. Through the windows, the lights of Salford Quays slide past — a modern skyline soundtracked by timeless classics.

It makes a fantastic night out for a birthday, a group of friends or anyone who likes their music with a bit of attitude. The atmosphere does half the work: put a boat full of people together with the greatest showman in rock 'n' roll history, and the result is a night that genuinely rocks.

The King's cruises are always in demand, so round up your fellow fans and book your spot early. Come and shake, rattle and roll with us on the water — because uh-huh-huh, this is one night you won't want to miss.
