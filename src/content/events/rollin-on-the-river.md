Big voice, bigger energy — Rollin' on the River brings the unstoppable force of Tina Turner to the Manchester Ship Canal for a night of pure rock 'n' soul. This is not a sit-back-and-listen kind of evening; from the first strut to the final encore, it is all attitude, all power and absolutely no holding back.

As the boat pushes out from Salford Quays, the anthems come rolling in. There is the slow-burn-to-explosion of "Proud Mary", the defiant swagger of "Simply the Best", the wall-of-sound drama of "River Deep Mountain High" and the stomping groove of "Nutbush City Limits". These are songs built to be belted at full volume, and before long the whole boat is on its feet, singing every word and dancing like nobody's watching.

With a fully licensed bar onboard and Manchester's illuminated waterfront gliding past the windows, the atmosphere builds and builds. Tina's music has a way of making everyone feel ten feet tall, and out on the water — lights shimmering on the canal — that feeling only gets bigger. It is a fantastic night out for a birthday, a girls' night or any group that likes their music loud, proud and full of heart.

Come ready to move, because this one rarely leaves anyone in their seat. Expect goosebumps, grins and a fair bit of air-punching by the time the final chorus lands.

Our tribute nights are hugely popular and tickets go quickly, so gather your crew and book ahead. You never do anything nice and easy — so roll on down to the river, and let's do it rough.
