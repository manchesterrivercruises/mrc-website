Grab your wand, pin on your wings and prepare for a spot of magic on the water. Wizards & Fairies transforms the Manchester Ship Canal into an enchanted world where little ones can cast spells, meet fairies and set sail with a cast of magical characters.

As soon as your young sorcerers and sprites step aboard at Salford Quays, the enchantment begins. There are spells to learn, songs to sing and characters to meet, all woven into a gentle cruise along the water that is perfectly pitched for little ones. Children are warmly encouraged to arrive in costume — the more sparkle and stardust, the better — and every child is part of the adventure.

For parents and carers, it is a chance to relax and watch the wonder unfold, with refreshments available onboard and a calm, easy pace throughout. There is no marching between attractions or standing in long queues — just a boat, a sprinkle of magic and a lot of wide-eyed smiles as the waterfront drifts gently past.

Wizards & Fairies is a lovely treat for a birthday, a school-holiday outing or a special family day. It is the kind of experience that fires little imaginations and stays with them long after the boat has moored — and it makes for some truly enchanting photos.

Our magical family cruises fill up fast during the holidays, so gather your little coven and book ahead. A world of wonder awaits — we'll see you on the water.
