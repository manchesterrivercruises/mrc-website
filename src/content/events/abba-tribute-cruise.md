Grab your flares, dust off your dancing shoes and get ready to be the Dancing Queen you were always meant to be. Our ABBA Cruise takes one of the most-loved songbooks in pop history and sets it loose on the water, for a night of pure, unashamed, sing-at-the-top-of-your-lungs joy.

From the moment you step aboard at Salford Quays, the mood is set. As the boat pushes out along the Manchester Ship Canal, the hits come thick and fast — "Mamma Mia", "Waterloo", "Super Trouper", "Take a Chance on Me" — every one a chorus you already know by heart. Before long the dancefloor is full, glasses are in the air, and the whole boat is moving as one.

It's the perfect night out for a birthday, a hen party, a work do or simply a brilliant excuse to get the group together. There's a fully licensed bar onboard to keep the drinks flowing, and the ever-changing backdrop of Manchester's illuminated waterfront gliding past the windows adds a bit of magic you just don't get on dry land.

ABBA's music has a way of bringing everyone together — all ages, all moods, no wallflowers allowed — and there's something special about belting out those anthems while the city lights shimmer on the water. Whether you're a lifelong super trouper or just here for the singalong, this is a feel-good night from start to finish.

Tickets for our ABBA nights go quickly, so gather your group and book early to avoid disappointment. Thank you for the music — now come and dance to it, right here on the Ship Canal.
