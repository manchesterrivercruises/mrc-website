Some nights out just hit different — and there's nothing quite like a proper dancefloor floating out on the water. Club Classics is our all-out party cruise: DJ locked in, bass rolling across the canal, and two hours of the anthems that made you fall in love with a big night out in the first place.

This is where the Ship Canal becomes the best dancefloor in the city. As the boat leaves Salford Quays, the DJ drops the first classic and it's game on — hands in the air, everyone singing the same words, that unmistakable feeling of a room (well, a boat) moving together. From soulful house to the piano-driven anthems and floor-fillers you can't help but shout back, the set is built for one thing: keeping you dancing from cast-off to the final track.

With a fully licensed bar onboard and Manchester's illuminated waterfront wrapping around the windows, the atmosphere builds all night. There's something about being out on the water — the lights reflecting off the canal, the city sliding by — that turns a great party into a proper occasion.

It's tailor-made for birthdays, group celebrations and anyone who'd rather be dancing than sitting down. Get your crew together, claim your spot on the floor early, and let the DJ do the rest.

Club Classics nights are one of our most popular party cruises and they sell fast, so don't leave it late. Bring the energy — we'll bring the tunes, the bar and the best view of the skyline in Manchester.
