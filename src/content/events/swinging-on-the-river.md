Pour a martini, straighten your tie and step aboard — Swinging on the River brings a little Rat Pack magic to the Manchester Ship Canal. It is a smooth, stylish night of swing standards and old-school glamour, the kind of evening that makes you feel like the coolest person in the room.

As the boat eases out from Salford Quays, the band lifts into the songbook that never goes out of fashion. There is the effortless charm of Sinatra — "Fly Me to the Moon", "The Way You Look Tonight" — the wink of Dean Martin, the swagger of Sammy Davis Jr, and all those big, brassy finishers that get the whole boat clicking its fingers. It is music to sway to, to sing along with, and to raise a glass to.

With a fully licensed bar onboard and Manchester's waterfront lights shimmering past the windows, the whole evening has a wonderfully classy feel. It is the perfect night out for a birthday or anniversary, a group who fancy something a bit different, or anyone who loves the timeless cool of the swing era.

Dress up or come as you are — either way, the music does the work. Expect toe-tapping from the first number, a few grand singalongs, and that unmistakable feel-good glow that only the classics can bring.

Our swing nights are a firm favourite and tickets don't hang around, so gather your group and book ahead. It's going to be a very good year — starting on the water.
