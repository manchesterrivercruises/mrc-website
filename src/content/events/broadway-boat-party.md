If you know every word to every showstopper, this one is for you. The Broadway Boat Party brings the drama, the belters and the sheer joy of musical theatre to the Manchester Ship Canal — a night where the whole boat becomes the chorus.

From the moment you step aboard at Salford Quays, the curtain is up. The set sweeps through the greatest numbers the West End and Broadway have to offer: the soaring defiance of "Defying Gravity", the roof-raising "This Is Me", the heartbreak of Les Mis, the sparkle of Mamma Mia and the wit of Hamilton. These are songs written to be sung at full volume — and out on the water, with a boatful of fellow theatre lovers, that is exactly what happens.

With a fully licensed bar onboard and Manchester's illuminated waterfront gliding past the windows, the atmosphere builds like a great second act. There are goosebump moments, big group singalongs and more than a few standing ovations before the night is through. It is a wonderful outing for a birthday, a group of friends, or anyone who has ever wished the whole world was a musical.

No triple-threat audition required — just bring your voice and your enthusiasm, and let the showstoppers carry you away. Whether you are front-row-at-the-theatre devoted or simply love a big tune, this is a feel-good night from overture to encore.

Our Broadway nights are hugely popular and tickets go fast, so gather your fellow fans and book ahead. It's showtime — on the water.
